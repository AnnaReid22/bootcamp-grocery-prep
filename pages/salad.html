A salad can be really yummy and healthy!

Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Veggies (Tomatoes, basil, peas, etc)
  • Chicken, if desired
  • Your favorite salad dressing

Instructions

  1. Wash your lettuce in a strainer.
  2. Shake excess water, add to bowl.
  3. Add toppings (veggies).
  4. Add the perfect amount of dressing.
  5. Your salad is ready!
  6. Yummy!
