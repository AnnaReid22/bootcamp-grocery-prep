Green Jello

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Crushed Pineapple
  • 1 Box Large Green Jello
  • 1 Cup Cottage Cheese
  • 1 Cup Whipped Cream

Instructions

  1. Drain and save pineapple juice for later.
  2. Boil and add two cups to Jello and dissolve. Add pineapple juice with water to total 2 cups additional liquid and add to hot Jello mixture.
  3. Put in fridge until almost set.
  4. Fold in Cottage Cheese and Whipped cream. Put in fridge to set.
