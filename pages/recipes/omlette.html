Omlette

Serving Size:
1

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 0.25 cup cheese (or substitute)
  • Spinach
  • Basil
  • 5 baby Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Stir eggs in a separate container.
  2. Pour mix onto hot pan, moving the eggs as necessary.
  3. Place cheese, spinach, and tomatoes on half of egg sheet.
  4. Fold other half over, cook until prepared.
