Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Basil
  • Sausage
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Mash up potatoes in a bowl.
  2. Add warm butter.
  3. Keep mashing.
  4. Heat up sausages from the store.
  5. Put mashed potatoes on the stove, heat up.
  6. Sprinkle basil/other seasonings on top.
  7. Your food is ready!
  8. Yummy!
