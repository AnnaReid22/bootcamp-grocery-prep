Sweet Potato Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Sweet Potatoes
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ Cup butter
  • ½ Cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/3 Cup Milk

Instructions

  1. Boil and mash potatoes. Mix in sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla and milk. Put in 9x13 baking pan.
  2. Topping: 1/3 cup melted butter, 1 Cup light brown sugar, ½ Cup chopped pecans, ½ cup flour.
  3. Mix topping ingredients. Sprinkle over potatoes. Bake 25 minutes in 350 degrees.
