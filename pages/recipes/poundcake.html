Pound Cake

Serving Size:
1

Ingredients

  • 0.75 Cup Milk
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1 lb butter
  • 3 Cups Sugar
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Cream butter, gradually add sugar beating well at medium speed. Add eggs (room temp) at a time beating after each addition. Add flour, alternately with milk-beginning and ending with flour. Mix just until blended, add flavorings.
  2. Pour batter into greased, floured 10‚Äù tube pan. Bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour 40 minutes or until wooden pick comes out clean.
  3. Cool cake in pan 10-15 minutes; remove cake and let cool on wire rack. Cover with berries or add chocolate icing.
