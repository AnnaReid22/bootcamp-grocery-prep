Chicken Curry

Serving Size:
1

Ingredients

  • 6 lbs Chicken
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Clove Garlic, chopped
  • 1 Pint Water
  • 2 Chicken Bouillon cube
  • 0.5 tsp. Curry Powder
  • 2 tbsp Sherry Wine
  • Salt/pepper

Instructions

  1. Bake Chicken with listed ingredients in a 350 degree oven for 1 ½ hours or until tender.
  2. Remove chicken and bone. To stock in roaster add the following: water, bouillon, curry powder, sherry. Cook until liquid bubbles. Thicken with 4 Tbsp. flour.
  3. Strain gravy into large saucepan. Add 1 can button mushrooms and juice and 1 can undiluted mushroom soup which has been beaten smooth. Blend over medium heat. Add chicken meat and heat.
  4. Just before serving, stir ½ cup commercial sour cream into chicken mixture. Serve in chafing dish with chived rice as side dish.
