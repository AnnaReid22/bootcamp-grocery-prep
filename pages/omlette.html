Omlette

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Veggies (Tomatoes, basil, etc)
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl.
  2. Stir eggs until they are all mixed together.
  3. Put a cooking pan on the stove (add nonstick cooking spray if necessary).
  4. Dump eggs onto cooking pan, scraping and stirring as they cook.
  5. Add cheese and preferred veggies.
  6. Put cooked omlette on a plate.
  7. Yummy!
