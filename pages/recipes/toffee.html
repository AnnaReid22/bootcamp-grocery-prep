Toffee

Serving Size:
1

Ingredients

  • 1 lb butter
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 16 oz Hershey’s chocolate bar broken in pieces
  • 2 cups chopped salted peanuts

Instructions

  1. Cook butter and brown sugar to 300 degrees, stirring constantly (use candy thermometer).
  2. Pour immediately into a buttered jelly roll pan.
  3. Lay broken up chocolate on bar top and let melt…then spread. Sprinkle with chopped nuts.
