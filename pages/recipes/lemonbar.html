Lemon Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • ½ Cup Butter
  • ¼ Cup Powdered Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 tbsps Lemon Juice
  • 2 tbsps Flour
  • 1 whole lemon
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1 Cup Sugar

Instructions

  1. Pat into 8x8 or 9x9 greased pan depending on thickness desired. Pat dough up on side edges a bit.
  2. Bake for 15 minutes at 350May double recipe and use 9x13 pan. Beat eggs, add lemon juice, flour, rind of whole lemon, baking powder, and sugar. Mix with a fork.
  3. Fill shell while hot and bake 25 minutes more at 350 degrees. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and cut while warm.
