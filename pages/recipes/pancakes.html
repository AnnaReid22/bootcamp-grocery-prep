Unique Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Â½ Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp. Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Put butter in 9x13 cake pan and melt by putting in oven.
  2. Mix the eggs, milk, flour, and salt. Pour in pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Serve with anything else you want or by itself.
