Almond Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ Cup All-Purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ Cup butter
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ½ cup sliced almonds maybe more

Instructions

  1. Stir together flour baking powder and salt.
  2. In a mixer bowl beat butter until softened.
  3. Add sugar and beat until fluffy.
  4. Add egg, almond extract and beat well. Add flour mixture and beat until well mixed.
  5. Divide dough into fourths. Form dough into 12 inch roll. Place 2 rolls 4-5 inches apart on a cookie sheet. Flatten until 3 to 4 inches wide. Brush flattened rolls with milk, sprinkle with almonds.
  6. Bake at 325 degrees for 12-13 minutes. While cookies are warm, cut cross wise at a diagonal 1 inch strips. Drizzle with almond icing Almond icing: 1 cup powdered sugar, 1/4 tsp almond extract and enough milk for icing of drizzling consistency.
