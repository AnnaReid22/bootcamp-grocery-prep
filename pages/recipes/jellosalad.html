Raspberry Jello

Serving Size:
1

Ingredients

  • 2 Packages of 3 oz Raspberry Jello
  • 2 Packages of Frozen Raspberries
  • 1 Cup Crushed Pineapple undrained
  • 8 oz sour cream

Instructions

  1. Dissolve Jello in 2 cups water.
  2. Add frozen raspberries to hot mixture and stir to defrost. Add pineapple.
  3. Put Â½ mixture in fridge to set. Then spread sour cream on top.
  4. Add remaining mixture on top and let set.
